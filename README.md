## Homemade Mate Lemonade

* 4 bags of Mate tea
* 250 ml hot water
* 1 Lime
* Sugar
* Some time
* This can be combined with for example ginger

## Mocktail

* Tonic water

## With Sparkling Wine

### Kir Berlin or Turbo Mate
* 1:1 Club Mate and Sparkling Wine, serve with crushed ice
* you can use another mixing ratio

### Oase
* 2 cl Mate
* 2 cl Gin

* Soda
